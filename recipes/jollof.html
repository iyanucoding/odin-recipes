Jollof Rice

Home

Description

To make jollof rice you need rice, tomatoes (in paste or crushed form), peppers, and a nigerian grandma.

Essentially, you prepare the stew with peppers and tomatoes. After mix them for a while at high temperature, you then add in the rice. Then you mix again and wait. Then the JOLLOF RICE is ready.

Ingredients

  • Tomato Paste
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Jalapeno Peppers
  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • Parboiled Rice
  • Onions
  • Basil Leaf

Steps

  1. Grab a nigerian grandmother
  2. Ask them for jollof nicely
  3. Enjoy your Nigerian cuisine!
